About our design

Team UAS NMIMS is a student group that aims at developing and fabricating Unmanned Aerial Systems. Through detailed studies and practical experience in the field, we believe in researching and developing highly advanced systems by integrating advanced modules. BlackPeafowl (hexacopter) being our UAV for the 16th AUVSI's SUAS International Competition secured 5th rank worldwide out of 70+ teams that participated from all over the world.

Our Journey

  • 2014

    Initial Study

    We started off with our initial study and training by building a quad copter at a training Institute in Kerala. Thereafter we undertook extensive research and built ourselves various RC controlled models, upon which we conducted various concept tests for a span of six months. This was our foundation.

  • 2015

    Our first drone, Scylla 2K16

    We began designing and developing our first ever fixed wing drone, Scylla 2K16. The drone was fabricated under the supervision of Industry experts and specialists.

  • 2015

    Workshops across India

    We have conducted our "DroneTech" workshops in Institutes such as NMIMS University and Manipal Institute of Technology along with other schools.

  • 2016

    International Event - AUVSI SUAS 2016

    We participated in the AUVSI SUAS 2016 competition held in Maryland,USA with our drone Scylla 2K16. Over 30 teams from all across the globe participated in this annual competition. We secured a Mission Rank 5 at the competition.

  • 2017

    Internation Event - AUVSI SUAS 2017

    We participated for the second time in competition with our new drone SkyKing'17. 56 teams from all across the globe participated in this annual competition. We secured a Mission Rank 28 at the competition.

  • 2018

    International Event - AUVSI SUAS 2018

    We participated for the third time in the 16th AUVSI SUAS 2018 competition held in Maryland,USA with our hexa-copter. Over 75 teams from all across the globe participated in this annual competition. We secured 5th rank worldwide and 1st amongst all Indian teams.
